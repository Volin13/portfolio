M-partner

M-partner, a fairly simple job - a landing page for my friend who is engaged in employment abroad. Despite the fact that the work is quite simple, I put a lot of effort into the design. It can be considered my first commercial project. I got an interesting experience buying a domain, deploying it, indexing the site in Google, etc. It was also interesting to work with meta tags and learn more about SEO.

M-partner header

Since the company's contacts are of the greatest importance on the entire site, I decided to highlight them as much as possible on the start page, next to the pulsating logo.

M-partner navigation menu

The navigation menu, the client wanted it to be visible all the time, so when clicking on the logo, the link would take the user to it.

M-partner about section

In general, this landing page has an exclusively informative purpose, the main content is a description of the company and an algorithm for interacting with them.
